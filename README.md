## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Uber ride data to uncover business insights related to customer behavior, revenue patterns, time trends, cancellations, and operational efficiency.

The goal of this project is to simulate a real-world data analyst workflow:
- Data Cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL-based analysis
- Business insights & recommendations
- Storytelling for decision-makers

---

## Business Objectives
- Identify peak demand hours
- Analyze revenue patterns
- Understand customer behavior
- Detect cancellation issues
- Evaluate location performance
- Optimize operational decisions

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains information about:
- Booking date and time
- Pickup and drop locations
- Ride distance
- Vehicle type
- Customer and driver ratings
- Cancellations
- Booking value (revenue)

## Key KPIs
- Total Rides
- Completed Rides
- Cancelled Rides
- Total Revenue
- Average Revenue per Ride
- Cancellation Rate
- Revenue by Location
- Revenue by Vehicle Type
- Customer Retention

---

## Key Insights
- Demand is concentrated in specific peak hours.
- A small number of users generate a large share of revenue.
- Certain locations and vehicle types outperform others.
- Cancellations represent a significant loss in revenue.
- Most users are one-time or occasional users.

---

## Recommendations
- Increase driver availability during peak hours.
- Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Investigate main cancellation reasons.
- Optimize driver allocation by location.
- Improve service quality to boost ratings.
